About The Competition

The SAE International Clean Snowmobile Challenge (CSC) is an engineering design competition for SAE college student members that seek to challenge students through the reengineering of an existing snowmobile in order to reduce emissions and noise. The teams use their modified snowmobiles to compete in a variety of events that provide similar tests to those seen in industry. The tests challenge the improvement of: emissions, noise, fuel economy/endurance, acceleration, handling, static display, cold start and design. Unlike other SAE collegiate completions, the CSC makes changes to the competition each year to present new engineering challenges. This allows students to explore different aspects of automotive practices, and to get a full overview of the vehicle. CSC is primarily an engine competition with an underlying theme of clean engineering.
