About RIT Clean Snowmobile

Overview

The RIT Clean Snowmobile is a competitive automotive club at the Rochester Institute of Technology. Our goal is to have a vehicle that can compete and succeed in the SAE Clean Snowmobile Competition. The team designs, builds, tests, and tunes a snowmobile for competitions. Doing this helps the members learn the most about automotive industry practices, and gain hands-on experience. Using advanced modeling, analysis software, intricate equipment for static and dynamic testing for validation, RIT Clean Snowmobile members are learning how to become high caliber engineers.

Mission Statement

To become the best Clean Snowmobile team in the world by increasing vehicle function in the areas of emissions, powertrain, dynamics, and kinematics.
